MPU6050 Constructor

Parameters
i2c- i2c peripheral used to commnicate with device.
full_scale- the specification maximum detectable acceleration. Allowed values are 2g, 4g, 8g, and 16g, where g reprsents the gravitational constent 9.8 m/s^2 (the user defined literals for this are 2_SG, 4_SG, 8_SG, and 16_SG). Setting this to a larger max acceleration results in being able to detect accelerations but loses resolution because -16g to 16g must span 12 bits of information. Where as 2g has a lower maximum detectable acceleration, but 12 bits of information.
address- Mpu6050 device address.
